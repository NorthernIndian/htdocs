Race Reports

I started writing these race reports to preserve the details of each race - what went right, what went wrong, and everything in between. And one day, I hope to share them with my kids, so they can look back and think, “Wow, dad was out there running, trying his best… and was still slow as hell. Poor old fucker.”

There isn't much detail on the older races because too much time has passed to remember the exact details.

Feel free to use a copy of my race report template or use the original from /u/herumph.
