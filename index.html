A short history

I grew up in Guelph, Ontario and whimsically moved to Calgary, Alberta in my early twenties. I fell into an electrical apprenticeship and transitioned to the world of automation after becoming a journeyman electrician. I have been working in the oilfield for the past decade which has been an interesting experience. I married my high school sweet heart and we have two beautiful children.
