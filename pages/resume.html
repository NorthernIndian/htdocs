Resume

In this version of my resume, I have included much more than just my work experience. It is a snapshot of my life experience and skills- both professional and personal. Consider it a birds eye view of the work I have done and continue to do.

Why?

I have seen many companies hire the wrong people over and over again. People who are terrible with personal finance are suddenly responsible for a massive budget. People who can't manage their personal relationships are suddenly in a management position. People who smoke and don't exercise are suddenly in charge of health and safety.

These people are hired not because they are qualified, they are hired because they impress they right person in the right moment at the right time. Fair enough - a lot of success in life is based on luck.

But if I were a hiring manager, I would put aside first impressions and focus on the big picture: Who is this person? What do they do? What are their life experiences?

Below is a copy of my personal resume where I try to answer those questions and give anyone looking for my skills a chance to gain insight.
